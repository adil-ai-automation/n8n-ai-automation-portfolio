# AI SEO Article Generator

## Overview

The AI SEO Article Generator is an n8n workflow that creates long-form, SEO-optimised blog articles using OpenAI. It generates structured, publication-ready content based on a target keyword, helping businesses improve their search engine visibility and content marketing efforts.

---

## Problem

Creating high-quality SEO content consistently is time-consuming and requires expertise in keyword optimisation, article structure, and search intent. Many businesses struggle to publish content regularly while maintaining quality.

---

## Solution

This workflow generates comprehensive, SEO-friendly articles from a single target keyword using AI.

The generated article includes:

- SEO-optimised title
- Engaging introduction
- Structured H2 and H3 headings
- Naturally integrated keywords
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- Strong conclusion
- Markdown formatting

---

## Business Value

This workflow helps businesses:

- Produce SEO content faster
- Improve search engine visibility
- Reduce content writing costs
- Maintain a consistent publishing schedule
- Generate high-quality blog articles at scale
